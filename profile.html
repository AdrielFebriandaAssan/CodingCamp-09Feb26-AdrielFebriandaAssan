About Us

Universitas Teknologi Yogyakarta or UTY is a university located in Daerah Istemewa Yogyakarta (DIY). UTY is established in 23 October 2002 by merging 3 university.

The three university that merged into UTY is:

  • Sekolah Tinggi Ilmu Ekonomi Yogyakarta (STIEYO)
  • Akademi Bahasa Asing Yogyakarta (ABAYO)
  • Sekolah Tinggi Manajemen Informatika dan Komputer (STMIK) Dharma Bangsa

Vision and Mission

Vision:

To become university that is excellent, adaptive, and innovative and contribute to educating the nation and help imrpove the community welfare.

Mission

  • Carry out high-quality university Tridharma, adaptive, innovative, and sustainable.
  • Produce graduates who are high-quality, have strong character, are adaptive and innovative, and are competitive.
  • To implement excellent, fair, and sustainable higher education management based on information technology.
  • To optimize the participation of all stakeholders to support the achievement of the vision.
